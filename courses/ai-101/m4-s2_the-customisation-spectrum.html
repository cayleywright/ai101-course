Module 4

The Customisation Spectrum

The gap between generic AI and AI that actually works for your business is the single biggest factor in whether it saves you time or wastes it. Everyone starts on the generic side. The question is how far across you need to go — and most people either don't go far enough or spend too much getting there.

There are five levels. Each one unlocks a different kind of control. Each one requires a different level of access and investment. Click a level on the dial to explore.

Level 1
In-chat prompts
Better prompts, custom instructions, conversation context. Everything you can do inside ChatGPT or Claude right now with no technical setup. This is where everyone starts.
Free, instant Everyone
▲ Most businesses are here

The five levels

Each level builds on the last. Most businesses start at level 1 and many stay there. The ones that need more move up deliberately, not because a vendor told them to.

1
In-chat prompts
Better prompts, custom instructions. Everything you can do inside ChatGPT or Claude right now, no technical setup. Free, instant.
2
API and developer access
System prompts, temperature control, structured outputs. What opens up when you move beyond the chat interface into building tools. Requires some technical capability or a developer.
3
Persistent knowledge
Project workspaces, knowledge bases, connected data sources. Giving AI access to your business information so it doesn't start from scratch every time. Ranges from free (uploading files to a project) to significant investment.
4
Changing model behaviour
Fine-tuning and model customisation. Teaching the model your specific patterns, style, or classification rules from your own data. Weeks of work, significant cost.
5
Building your own model
Training from scratch. Millions of dollars, ML engineering team, months of compute. Almost no business will ever do this.
